Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and our commitment to your security.

ZERO-SERVER PROCESSING: Most tools on AllToolsFree use Client-Side processing. Your files never touch our servers.

1. Information Collection

We believe in minimal data footprint. Unlike other utility portals, we do not require registration or account creation. We do not collect:

  • Names, Email addresses, or Phone numbers.
  • Uploaded files (PDFs, Images, Resumes).
  • Sensitive financial data entered into our calculators.

2. Cookies & Advertising

To keep our services 100% free, we partner with Google AdSense. Google uses cookies (like the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

3. Local Browser Storage

Some tools may use localStorage or SessionStorage to save your progress (e.g., Resume Builder or Invoice Maker). This data remains on your physical device and is not accessible by us.

4. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to know that your data is not being sold or stored.
