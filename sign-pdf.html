What is a Digital Signature?

A digital signature is a hand-drawn electronic mark used to verify documents. At AllToolsFree by Jiban70, we ensure your signature is generated in high resolution with a transparent background. This means you can place it over any PDF, Word document, or image without a white box blocking the text.

Is it secure? Yes. Your signature is drawn directly on your browser's canvas. We never store, see, or transmit your signature to any server. It is 100% private and secure.
