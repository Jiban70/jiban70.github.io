What are Open Graph Meta Tags?

Open Graph meta tags are snippets of code that control how URLs are displayed when shared on social media. They were originally introduced by Facebook but are now used by almost every major platform, including LinkedIn and Discord.

Why Preview Your Social Link?

A link shared without a title or image looks suspicious and unprofessional. By optimizing your og:image and og:description, you can increase your Click-Through Rate (CTR) by up to 250%. This tool helps you visualize that impact before you publish your changes.

Best Practices for Images

  • Resolution: Use 1200 x 630 pixels for best results on high-dpi screens.
  • Format: JPEG or PNG are preferred.
  • Branding: Place your logo in a safe area so it doesn't get cropped on different devices.
