What is a Case Converter?

A Case Converter is a handy tool that allows you to change the capitalization of text with a single click. Whether you accidentally typed a paragraph with Caps Lock on or need to format a title for an article, our tool handles the heavy lifting for you.

Supported Text Transformations:

  • Title Case: Capitalizes every word except for small prepositions (of, the, and).
