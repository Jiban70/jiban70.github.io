The Perfect Instagram Story Size

Instagram Stories require a specific aspect ratio of 9:16. Our tool ensures your graphics are exported at 1080 x 1920 pixels, which is the gold standard for high-definition mobile viewing. This prevents Instagram from stretching or pixelating your content.

3 Tips for Better IG Stories

  • Rule of Thirds: Keep your main text in the center. Avoid placing text too high (where your profile info is) or too low (where the 'Send Message' bar sits).
  • High Contrast: Use dark backgrounds with white text for maximum readability while users are fast-scrolling.
  • Call to Action: Use the Story Maker to create "New Post" or "Link in Bio" alerts that grab attention instantly.
