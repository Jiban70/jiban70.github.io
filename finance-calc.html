How is your loan calculated?

Our tool uses the Standard Reducing Balance Amortization Formula: $$EMI = \left[ P \cdot r \cdot (1 + r)^n \right] / \left[ (1 + r)^n - 1 \right]$$ where $P$ is the principal, $r$ is the monthly interest rate, and $n$ is the number of months. This ensures your calculation matches exactly what banks use.
