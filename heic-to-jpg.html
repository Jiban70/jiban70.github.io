What is HEIC?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is the default photo format for Apple iPhones. While it saves space, it won't open on many Windows PCs or Android phones. AllToolsFree by Jiban70 provides a secure, no-upload solution to convert your private memories into the universally accepted JPG format.
