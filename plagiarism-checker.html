What is Keyword Density?

Keyword density is the percentage of times a keyword appears in your text compared to the total word count. For optimal SEO in 2025, you should aim for a density between 1% and 2%. Our live analyzer helps you avoid "Keyword Stuffing," ensuring your content remains natural and reader-friendly.
