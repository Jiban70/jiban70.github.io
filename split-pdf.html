Securely Split PDF Pages Online

Managing large documents is difficult. The AllToolsFree PDF Splitter allows you to isolate exactly what you need. Whether it's a single page from a contract or a specific chapter from an eBook, our tool handles the heavy lifting without sacrificing document quality.

How it Works:

  • Privacy Guaranteed: Unlike other PDF tools, we use pdf-lib technology which processes the file inside your browser's memory. No data is ever uploaded to a server.
  • Custom Ranges: You have full control. You can extract non-sequential pages (1, 4, 9) or sequential blocks (10-20) in a single action.
  • Zero Cost: No subscriptions, no watermarks, and no daily limits.

Page Range Syntax:

To extract pages 1, 2, 3, and 5, you would enter: 1-3, 5. Ensure you do not exceed the total number of pages in your original document.
