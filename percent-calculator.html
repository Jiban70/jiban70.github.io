Professional Percentage Math in 2026

Percentages are the universal language of comparison. Whether you are calculating Year-over-Year (YoY) growth for a business report or determining the final price of an item after a discount, accuracy is paramount. Our Percentage Engine provides instant results using 64-bit precision logic.

The Growth & Decay Formula

Calculating the difference between two values as a percentage is essential for tracking progress or loss. The mathematical relationship is expressed as:

$$ \text{Percentage Change} = \left( \frac{\text{New} - \text{Old}}{|\text{Old}|} \right) \times 100 $$

Did you know? A 10% increase followed by a 10% decrease does not return you to your original number. If you start with 100, add 10%, you get 110. Subtracting 10% from 110 leaves you with 99. This is known as the "base effect," and our calculator ensures these nuances are never missed.
