Why Keyword Density Matters in 2026

In the modern era of semantic search, search engines focus on "user intent." However, Keyword Density remains a fundamental metric for determining the primary subject of your content. Maintaining a healthy ratio ensures your page is indexed correctly without being flagged for keyword stuffing.

Ideal Ratios for Search Success

While there is no "perfect" percentage, SEO experts in 2026 generally recommend these density ranges for maximum visibility:

  • Primary Keyword: 1.5% - 2.5%
  • Secondary Keywords: 0.5% - 1.2%
  • LSI Phrases: 0.2% - 0.5%

The Danger of Keyword Stuffing

Over-optimizing your content (usually anything over 3% density) can lead to an "over-optimization penalty." This tool automatically highlights keywords that exceed the 2.8% safety threshold in red, allowing you to edit them before publishing.
