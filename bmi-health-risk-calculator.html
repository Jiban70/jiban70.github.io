What is Body Mass Index?

The BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measurement of a personâ€™s leanness or corpulence based on their height and weight, and is intended to quantify tissue mass. It is widely used as a general indicator of whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height.

BMI Classifications:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Healthy: BMI 18.5 - 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 - 29.9
  • Obese: BMI > 30
