Why Use an Online Character Counter?

Whether you are crafting the perfect SEO Meta Title or writing a viral tweet on X, character limits are everywhere. Using an online character counter helps you stay within platform boundaries, ensuring your content isn't cut off or truncated.

SEO Character Limits (2026 Standards)

To rank effectively on Google, your snippets must be readable. Our tool helps you monitor the two most important SEO limits:

  • SEO Title: Best kept under 60 characters to avoid truncation in mobile and desktop search results.
  • Meta Description: Ideally between 140 and 160 characters to maximize click-through rates.

Social Media Content Optimization

For platforms like X (formerly Twitter), the 280-character limit is strict. On **LinkedIn**, while you have more room, the first 140 characters act as your "hook." Our real-time tracker allows you to see exactly how close you are to these limits as you type.
