Modern Tipping Etiquette (2026 Standards)

Tipping cultures vary globally, but in many regions, the standard has shifted towards higher percentages due to rising costs. While 15% used to be the baseline, 18-20% is now considered the standard for good service.

How We Calculate the Split

The calculation is simple but precise. We take your subtotal, calculate the gratuity, and then divide the grand total by the number of people in your party. This ensures even distribution down to the cent.

Tipping Checklist:

  • Check for "Auto-Gratuity": Some restaurants automatically add a tip for parties of 6 or more.
  • Tip on Pre-tax or Post-tax? Traditionally, tips are calculated on the pre-tax subtotal.
  • Exceptional Service: For outstanding hospitality, 25% is a generous way to show appreciation.
