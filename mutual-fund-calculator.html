Strategic Mutual Fund Planning in 2026

Building wealth through mutual funds requires a clear understanding of the difference between **Systematic Investment Plans (SIP)** and **Lumpsum** investments. While Lumpsum investments benefit from a longer duration of compounding on the entire capital, SIPs allow investors to benefit from Rupee Cost Averaging, reducing the impact of market volatility.

The Power of Compounding

Compounding is the process where the value of an investment increases because the earnings on an investment, both capital gains and interest, earn interest as time passes. Our 2026 calculator uses the standard future value formula:

$$FV = P \times \frac{(1 + r)^n - 1}{r} \times (1 + r)$$
