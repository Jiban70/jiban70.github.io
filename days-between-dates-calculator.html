How to Use the Date Duration Calculator

Calculating the days between two dates is a common requirement for project management, legal tracking, and personal countdowns. Our tool provides a breakdown of the duration into days, weeks, months, and even productive business days (workdays).

The Logic Behind Date Calculations

We use the Unix timestamp conversion method to ensure absolute accuracy. The formula calculates the total milliseconds between two points in time and converts them into days:

Days = |Date2 - Date1| / (1000 * 60 * 60 * 24)
