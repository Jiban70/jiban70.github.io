How is your age calculated?

The AllToolsFree Age Calculator uses the chronological method to determine the time elapsed between your birth date and the current date. Unlike basic calculators, our algorithm by Jiban70 accounts for leap years and the varying number of days in each month to give you a result that is 100% accurate for legal and official documentation.

Why use this tool?

Many government forms, insurance applications, and school admissions require your exact age in years and months. Instead of calculating manually, our tool provides a full "Life Breakdown," showing you exactly how many weeks and days you have lived. It also features a "Next Birthday Countdown" so you never miss a celebration!
