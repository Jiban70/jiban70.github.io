Understanding VAT Calculations

Value Added Tax (VAT) is a multi-stage consumption tax. Whether you are a business owner calculating invoices or a consumer checking a receipt, understanding how to add or remove VAT is essential for financial accuracy.

Calculating Inclusive vs. Exclusive

There are two primary ways to look at VAT:

  • VAT Exclusive: You have the base price and need to add tax on top.
  • VAT Inclusive: The price you see is the total, and you need to find out how much of that is tax.
// To Add VAT (20%):
Total = Net * 1.20

// To Remove VAT (20%):
Net = Total / 1.20
